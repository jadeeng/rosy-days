rosy days founding and story

Upon ideation, rosy days was overlooked in favor of subjects that were easier to broach. Even though it was brought up in a group of fairly forward thinking women, it was thought that the topic of mensturation was not one suitable to address in such a short time-- we only had a week to create the application, and we wanted to do it justice.

Then, months later, after dozen of encounters with women who needed feminine hygiene products and being in need of them personally, one of our founders realized that while it was important to not take the topic lightly, it was more important that our product be available to the public. Women need period products. It's not a luxury, it's an urgent, anxiety riddled necessity that people can't afford.

Thus, rosy days was born in a twenty-four hour period of time, at RoseHack. While we don't hope to have addressed the entirety of the subject, we hope that our product allows women in the United States to have access to menstruation products whenever they need it, whether it's for financial based reasons, or for women who don't want to discreetly ask fifteen different women in order to get a feminine hygiene product.

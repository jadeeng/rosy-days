Your Help Matters

Referring to menstruation in any capacity is taboo. When the word "period" is spoken in public, it nearly almost refers to the type of punctuation that ends a sentence. It's fitting, then, that we end the stigma around one of the most base body functions.

Around the world, there are 1.2 billion people with female genatalia who lack access to basic sanitation and hygiene products. Your donation can help them have access to what should be a basic human right.

Specifically, in the United states, there are still 32* states that still consider feminine hygiene products a "luxury," which makes these already expensive products even more unattainable for those who are struggling financially. "

*5 of the states that don't implement a luxury tax have no sales tax at all.
